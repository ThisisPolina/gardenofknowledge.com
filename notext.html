Welcome to your student guide to CMRU

We created this website prototype to help and prepare fellow students studying at the Wiang Bua Campus of Chiang Mai Rajabhat University. This site is translated into English, Thai, Burmese, and Chinese to reach as many people as possible. The information here comes from official sources and helpful students, but please fact-check in case anything changes.

Disclaimers

This site was made as a project for social engineering. We try to update as much as possible, but don't forget we're busy people. Always fact-check. We are not officially affiliated with CMRU.
