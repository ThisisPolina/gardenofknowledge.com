Chiang Mai Rajabhat University (27th/Memorial Building)

This is the main building where CMRU students study. IC-CMRU students usually come here for the 4th floor (International Affairs and Language Center). There are 15 floors in this building.

1st Floor

The 1st floor is for the Dean’s Office, Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences.

2nd Floor

The 2nd floor is for the President’s lounge.

3rd Floor

The 3rd floor is for conference rooms.

4th Floor

The 4th floor includes the Language Center and the Office of International Relations.
The Office of International Relations is located on the 4th floor of the 27th/Memorial Building. International students come here to handle visa matters and prepare required documents. The staff mostly speak English. They cannot assist with visas unrelated to studies or personal issues.
The Language Center is located on the 4th floor of the 27th/Memorial Building. Students (usually CMRU) come here for language training and exams.

5th Floor

The 5th floor is for the Office of General Education and Arts and Sciences.

6th Floor

The 6th floor is for educational standards, technology office, and classrooms.

7th Floor

The 7th floor is for the Mae Hong Son Campus Office.

8th Floor

The 8th floor includes tourism, hotel management, law, and public administration programs.

9th Floor

The 9th floor includes language programs such as Chinese, Korean, French, and English.

10th Floor

The 10th floor includes multiple humanities and social science programs.

11th Floor

The 11th floor is for Master’s and graduate studies.

12th–13th Floor

Graduate studies.

14th Floor

The 14th floor is for Airotec.
