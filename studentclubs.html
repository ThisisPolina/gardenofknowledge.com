Student Clubs Information

Q: What are the requirements to run or be in a student club?
A: Depends on the club. We think you need at least 7 people to start a club. You can start a student club during a student-club registration period, which is usually announced at Inter-CMRU's Facebook Page. You will need to fill out a form.
Q: What does each role in the student club do?
A: We suspect the President leads the club, the Vice President helps the President, the Secretary manages the club’s budget, and Public Relations promotes the club.
Q: Can you join student clubs at another college (IC-CMRU or CMRU)?
A: We don't know.
Q: Can you create a student club that already exists?
A: We don't know.
