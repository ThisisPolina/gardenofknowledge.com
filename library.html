CMRU Library

The library is located near the entrance of the campus and has 8 floors. The library is open Monday to Sunday from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM (Wiang Bua Campus) and Monday to Friday for the Mae Rim Campus. It is closed on public and university holidays. Many students come here to study or relax after lessons.
There are study areas outside, lockers, vending machines, and a copy shop on the left, with a small shop next to it. Toilets are located down the hallway between the library and Digital CMRU. You’ll need your student ID card to access the upper floors.
The main services the library offers to students, staff, and the public include borrowing and returning books, online renewals, study rooms for group work, research help, and access to computers, Wi-Fi, and databases. There’s also an Edutainment Zone where you can watch movies or use streaming services, and even a board game area.
Some special sections include law resources, finance and investment (SET Corner), ASEAN information, fiction books, children’s books, Lanna (Northern Thailand) resources, Middle East studies, research reports, theses, and government publications. There is also a Green Corner focused on environmental topics and sustainability.
Thai is mostly spoken, but some staff can speak English.
