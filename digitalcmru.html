Digital CMRU Building

This building has 4 floors and is located to the right of the library. If your exam room shows INC11 or INC12, you will need to go to the first floor of this building. The first floor has two computer rooms for digital self-learning (INC11, INC12). The second floor has a computer lab (INC21), a multimedia production room (INC22), an information system development office (INC23), and a computer repair service center (INC24). The third floor has the Digital Education Office (INC32) which handles general services and administration, finance and accounting, training and academic services, teaching and learning innovation, and computer network management (E-mail, Microsoft Teams, WiFi, VPN, Web Hosting), and a meeting room "Euang Sai Song Saeng" with 40 seats (INC31). The fourth floor has a mini theater/movie theater with 80 seats (INC41), an FM radio station 88.5 MHz (INC42), and a mini studio/editing room (INC43).
