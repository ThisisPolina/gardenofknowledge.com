Digital CMRU Building

This building has 4 floors and is located to the right of the library. If your exam room shows INC11 or INC12, you will need to go to the first floor of this building.

1st Floor

The first floor has two computer rooms for digital self-learning (INC11, INC12).

2nd Floor

The second floor has a computer lab (INC21), a multimedia production room (INC22), an information system development office (INC23), and a computer repair service center (INC24).

3rd Floor

The third floor has the Digital Education Office (INC32) which handles general services and administration, finance and accounting, training and academic services, teaching and learning innovation, and computer network management, and a meeting room "Euang Sai Song Saeng" with 40 seats (INC31).

4th Floor

The fourth floor has a mini theater (INC41), FM radio station 88.5 MHz (INC42), and a mini studio/editing room (INC43).
