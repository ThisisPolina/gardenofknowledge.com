Student Development Division

It is located in the 1st building, not the 6th building anymore.
IC-CMRU and CMRU students come here to ask questions about scholarships (e.g. Government Student Loan Fund), student activities, student loan issues, and other related information, as well as to apply for scholarships. The staff mostly speak Thai and cannot help with course registration or other course-related matters.
If you cannot get a letter to open a bank account on the 7th floor, you will be given a slip to take to this building. You will then need to go to the Finance Office to pay 20 baht and come back here for the letter.
